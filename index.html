What We Do?

Our Services

Exterior Washing

Exterior washing at Car Care Spa Palampur includes a meticulous cleaning of your vehicle's surface, removing dirt, grime, and contaminants to restore its shine and protect the paint.

Interior Washing

Interior washing at Car Care Spa Palampur involves a deep cleaning of your car's interior, including vacuuming, upholstery cleaning, and sanitizing surfaces to ensure a fresh, spotless, and comfortable environment.

Vacuum Cleaning

Vacuum cleaning at Car Care Spa Palampur thoroughly removes dust, dirt, and debris from your car's seats, carpets, and hard-to-reach areas, leaving the interior spotless and refreshed.

Interior Drycleaning

Interior dry cleaning at Car Care Spa Palampur offers a comprehensive cleaning of your car's upholstery, carpets, and roof liner, removing stains, dirt, and odors without the use of excessive water, ensuring a fresh and spotless interior.

Window Wiping

Window wiping at a car care spa involves cleaning the vehicle's glass surfaces using specialized solutions and tools to remove dirt and smudges. This process ensures a crystal-clear finish, enhancing visibility and the overall look of the car.

Wet Cleaning

Wet cleaning at a car care spa refers to the thorough washing of the vehicle's exterior using water and specialized cleaning solutions. This method effectively removes dirt, mud, and contaminants, ensuring a deep clean. Wet cleaning often includes rinsing, applying foam, and hand-washing to prevent scratches, followed by drying to leave a spotless finish. It's essential for maintaining the vehicle's appearance and protecting the paintwork.

Detailing

Car detailing includes rubbing and polishing to eliminate imperfections, applying ceramic coating for long-lasting protection and shine, and installing paint protection film (PPF) to guard against scratches and chips. Together, these services enhance the vehicle’s appearance while ensuring its paint remains in excellent condition.

Alloy Cleaning

Alloy cleaning at a car care spa involves meticulously removing dirt, brake dust, and grime from alloy wheels. Using specialized cleaners and brushes, this process restores the wheels' shine and protects against corrosion. Regular alloy cleaning not only enhances the vehicle's appearance but also helps maintain the integrity of the wheels.
